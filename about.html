Our Story

Black Hat was founded in 1997 with a single goal in mind: to provide the most technical and relevant information security research to the global security community. Since then, we have grown into a renowned cybersecurity event series, hosting conferences and trainings in the United States, Europe, and Asia.

Our mission is to bring together the best minds in the industry, providing a platform for elite security researchers and trainers to share their knowledge and expertise. We strive to inspire professionals at all career levels, fostering growth and collaboration among academia, researchers, and leaders in the public and private sectors.
